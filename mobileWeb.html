Good Design Principles for Mobile Web Development

What is Mobile Web Development?

Mobile web development is web development focused on developing a site to be viewed on mobile browsers. This can involve working through the use cases and functional/design/technological things that can come with those use cases (A usage scenario for a piece of software; often used in the plural to suggest situations where a piece of software may be useful. A potential scenario in which a system receives an external request and responds to it).

Mobile Design Principles

Content Prioritization

The human attention span is short. 8 seconds to be exact. this makes it extremely important that you grab your userâ€™s attention within the first few seconds of interaction with your mobile webpage layout. make sure to display essential content and functionality that the user needs. the limited space that is available on small screen makes every additional element you add overwhelming to your users.

Make Navigation Intuitive

users should be able to navigate through your mobile web page layout via clear pathways and be able to complete all primary tasks without requiring any explanations. users can lack the patience to try to work through a complex step to get what they want. if it takes too much time or effort to discover how to navigate through your product, chances are you are just going to lose your users.

Touchscreen Target Sizes

people interact with touch screens using their fingers. the important part is to make the interface elements big enough to capture these actions. people use their thumbs more often than any other finger while using their smartphones. small touch targets can be frustrating because that can require more accuracy and could be prone to errors. a touch target size of 9mm/34px with a minimum touch target size of 7mm/26px is ideal.

Provide User Control

making people feel like they are in control of their actions is what you want to give to them. allow users to make their own decisions and to personalize their journeys. by changing settings, controlling notifications, and cancelling actions. you provide the user with a sense of control of the system. when the user needs to know what is going one, let them know.
